Chieh is an visual artist born in Taiwan. Currently, she is a MFA Computer Arts student at School of Visual Arts and living in New York City.
Changed her life path from Economics background with working experiences in related fields, Chieh dived into the vast world of motion graphics. Chieh has worked on projects ranging from multimedia commercials to title sequence and infomation motion design. Her projects include 2D and 3D artworks supported by emotional storytelling. She especially likes to convey something informative and entertaining through motion design. When she is not designing, Chieh enjoys hanging out with her family and experimenting cooking with her husband.
